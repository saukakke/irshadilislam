About Us

Welcome to Irshadil Islamiyyah, located in the serene surroundings of Titin Makabarta (Sabuwa) Hayin Dogo, Samaru, Zaria. Our institution stands as a beacon of Islamic knowledge and enlightenment, dedicated to nurturing hearts and minds in the light of the Quran and Sunnah.

At Irshadil Islamiyyah, we pride ourselves on our commitment to providing quality Islamic education to our students. Our esteemed scholars bring years of experience and expertise to the classroom, guiding students on a journey of spiritual growth and moral development.

Our curriculum is designed to encompass a wide range of topics, including Quranic studies, Hadith, Islamic jurisprudence, and the life of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him). Through engaging lectures, interactive discussions, and practical application, students gain a deep understanding of Islamic principles and values that they can apply to their daily lives.

But our mission extends beyond the walls of the classroom. We strive to cultivate a strong sense of community and brotherhood among our students, fostering an environment of mutual respect, compassion, and unity. Through extracurricular activities, community service projects, and spiritual retreats, we encourage students to develop a holistic understanding of their faith and to become active contributors to society.

At Irshadil Islamiyyah, we believe that education is not just about acquiring knowledge but also about embodying the teachings of Islam in every aspect of our lives. Our ultimate goal is to empower our students to become righteous individuals who uphold the values of justice, integrity, and compassion, thereby making a positive impact on the world around them.

Join us at Irshadil Islamiyyah and embark on a journey of spiritual growth, academic excellence, and personal transformation. Together, let us strive to uphold the timeless principles of Islam and to build a brighter future for generations to come.

Our Mission

Our media team, "Irshad Media Team," is committed to informing, entertaining, and engaging audiences through diverse channels of communication. Our mission is to shape perspectives and inspire minds by delivering high-quality content that resonates with people from all walks of life. We aim to be a trusted source of information and entertainment, fostering meaningful connections within our communities. Our target audience includes tech-savvy millennial and Gen Z individuals who are passionate about staying informed, entertained, and socially conscious. We also cater to professionals seeking industry insights and enthusiasts interested in niche topics like sustainability, technology, and culture. At Irshad Media Team, we uphold key values of integrity, accuracy, creativity, and inclusivity in all aspects of our work. We prioritize honesty and transparency in our reporting, strive for accuracy in our content, embrace creativity in storytelling, and champion inclusivity by amplifying diverse voices and perspectives. Our platforms encompass a wide range of mediums, including our website, social media channels (Instagram, Twitter and Facebook), podcasts, videos on YouTube, and interactive forums for community engagement. Through these platforms, we deliver dynamic and engaging content that informs, entertains, and inspires our audience, fostering a vibrant and interactive media ecosystem.
